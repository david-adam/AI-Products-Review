Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

1. What We Do

ProductLens AI provides free, AI-powered product insights and reviews. Our service:

  • Discovers trending products from various sources
  • Generates AI-powered product summaries and insights
  • Provides honest, data-driven product recommendations
  • Tracks prices and notifies users of significant changes
Free Service: ProductLens AI is completely free to use. There are no paid subscriptions, no premium tiers, and no paywalls.

2. What We Don't Collect

We believe in privacy by design. Here's what we don't do:

2.1. No User Accounts

You do not need to register, create an account, or log in to use ProductLens AI. There is no authentication system, no user profiles, and no login functionality.

2.2. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Physical addresses or location data
  • Payment information or financial data
  • Social media profile information
  • User-generated content or comments

2.3. No Subscriptions

There are no newsletters, no email subscriptions, and no marketing communications to opt into or out of.

3. How Our Content Works

3.1. AI-Powered Automation

ProductLens AI uses artificial intelligence to automate content discovery and initial analysis. Our AI systems:

  • Scan and identify trending products across multiple sources
  • Extract product specifications, pricing, and key features
  • Generate initial product summaries and insights
  • Categorize products by type, price range, and use case

3.2. Human-in-the-Loop Review

Humans are involved in the process. While AI handles automation, our team:

  • Reviews AI-generated content for accuracy and quality
  • Curates product selections to ensure relevance and value
  • Verifies key information before publication
  • Adds contextual insights that AI may miss
Our Approach: We combine AI efficiency with human oversight. AI helps us scale and discover products, while humans ensure quality, accuracy, and helpfulness.

3.3. Accuracy Disclaimer

While we strive for accuracy through both AI and human review, product information changes frequently. Always verify product details, pricing, and availability directly on the retailer's website before making a purchase.

We are transparent about this because:
